You can use Lockify right now in "Guest Mode," i.e. without registering.

  • Your confidential message will be encrypted on your machine.
  • You'll be given a Lockify Link containing the only copy of the decryption key (Lockify only ever has the encrypted data, but never the key).
  • Share the Link with your desired recipient. If they pass whatever identity verification you require, they will be able to download and decrypt the message.
  • The Link will expire after 1 viewing or 3 days, whichever comes first. At that time the encrypted data will be permanently deleted from our server.

Labeling your Lockify Links will help you to tell them apart in both your email notifications, as well as in your "Track Links" interface.

So that you yourself can see the labels, they're not end-to-end encrypted. This means the server can "see" your labels as well, however your recipients never can.

By default, Lockify protects your private info using 256-bit AES end-to-end encryption. This produces Lockify Links that are 65 characters long. For example:

https://lockify.com/d#p4GdvihjObnsx1FvzvOYcCvUiCR3Fq15AfkDIBSksnI

Because we recommend that you don't shorten your Links, we offer the option to use a weaker level of encryption — 128-bit. This produces shorter Links that are 44 characters long. For example:

https://lockify.com/d#3jiBNAtSvPh0zIq5TRsB6w

If you don't let your recipient use the Lockify web app to access the Lockify Link, they will only be able to use the Chrome browser, and will need to install the Lockify Chrome App or Extension.

While providing extra security, this requirement might prevent some recipients from successfully accessing your Lockify Link.
